About me

Just your typical developer obsessed with debugging one line of code at a time.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Jquery, Bootstrap and Angular JS are my closest friends but I also hang out with NodeJs, MongoDB, ReactJs and Git sometimes too.

Like that sweet Boy Band in your dusty iPod, the hot new language you know today might be panhandling the streets of SF in six months, so it’s important to remain curious and learn new frameworks, languages and ways to borrow from those geniuses on Stack Overflow.

Since I started developing, I have fallen in love with the editing, problem solving, and the designing aspects that come with making cool creations. This also means I cannot wait to take my developer talents to a company where I can add to its growth while having a dynamic learning experience.
